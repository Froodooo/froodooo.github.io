--- title: Basic Principles for Business Development and Growth layout: page comments: false ---

Unless you start practicing and become competent in something, you'll never start earning.

Learning must come first, earning comes second.

  • Do something because you know you can learn from it. Then earning may even come automatically without you really trying.
  • It's like writing online, where you write about a topic to learn more about it yourself. Then eventually, these writings could let you earn (money or something else, like a job).
