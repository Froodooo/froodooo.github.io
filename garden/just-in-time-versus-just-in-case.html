--- title: Just In Time versus Just In Case layout: page comments: false ---

Our education system is built on a Just In Case system. Schools learn us about a wide variety of topics, just in case we need it somewhere in the future.

What works better is Just In Time learning. Start to learn about something when you know you will need it.
