How to write rubrics?

Use the "I"-form in rubrics to make them coherent.

Omit the use of "I have", "I do" and similar phrasings. Use Bloom's taxonomy instead.

Lower-level rubrics automatically transfer to higher-level rubrics. No need for repetition.
